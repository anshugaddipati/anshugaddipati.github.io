The Story Behind the Strategist

About Me

A journey shaped by people, leadership, and an obsession with making technology solve real human problems.

I'm Anshu Gaddipati — a Computer Science graduate from KL University, specialised in Cyber Security and Blockchain Technology. My path was shaped not just by technology, but by people, leadership, and the belief that the most powerful thing you can do with technology is direct it at real human challenges.

During my B.Tech years, I threw myself into university life beyond the classroom. As Chief Organiser of Samyak and Surabhi — two of KL University's largest annual fests — I was responsible for venue logistics, stall allocations, budgeting, crowd coordination, and real-time decision-making across thousands of attendees. These roles gave me something no syllabus could: the ability to lead under pressure, keep teams aligned, and deliver when it truly counted.

"The moment I organised the SWEEP voter awareness programme and invited IAS Officer Ms. Geetanjali Sharma, I witnessed how technology and governance can intersect to create real social impact — and that became my direction."

My internship at Efftronics Pvt. Ltd. brought everything together. Working in the R&D division on a live government project for the Vijayawada Police Commissionerate under IPS Commissioner S.V. Rajashekhar Babu — I was defining project scope, selecting algorithms, managing risk, and presenting impact metrics to senior officials. I wasn't just building systems. I was thinking like a project manager.

Today I am on a focused path toward becoming a Business Analyst and Project Manager — someone who bridges technical capability with strategic business outcomes, and believes the best projects don't just ship on time — they change how people live and work.
